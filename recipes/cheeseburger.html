Cheeseburger Recipe

To make hamburger patties, just mix the ground beef, egg, bread crumbs, evaporated milk, and spices and seasonings in a bowl using your hands. Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds ground beef
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 3/4 tablespoons evaporated milk
  • 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
